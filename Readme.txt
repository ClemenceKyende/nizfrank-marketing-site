NizFrank Marketing Consultant - Website


Welcome to the official website repository of NizFrank Marketing Consultant. This platform is designed to provide expert marketing strategies, branding solutions, SEO optimization, and social media growth services.

 Project Overview
The NizFrank Marketing Consultant website is a fully responsive, user-friendly, and performance-optimized platform that delivers professional marketing consultancy services. The website is designed to engage visitors, showcase expertise, and generate leads effectively.

Features
 Homepage – A compelling introduction to the company’s mission and services.
 About Us – Company values, approach, and credentials.
 Services – Digital marketing, SEO, branding, social media, PPC, and automation.
 Portfolio/Case Studies – Success stories with measurable results.
 Testimonials – Client feedback and social proof.
 Blog – Insights, tips, and trends in digital marketing.
 Contact Page – Inquiry form, business details, and call-to-action.
 Newsletter Subscription – Email sign-up for updates and marketing tips.
 Fully Responsive – Optimized for all devices and screen sizes.
 Technologies Used
 Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5
 Backend: PHP (For form handling)
 JavaScript: Swiper.js, AOS.js, Bootstrap.js
 Email Handling: Formspree
 Icons: Bootstrap Icons
 Fonts: Google Fonts (Open Sans, Marcellus)
 SEO Optimization: Meta descriptions, keywords, and structured data
